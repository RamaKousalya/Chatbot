# ⚖️ Legal AI Assistant Chatbot

## 📌 Overview

Legal AI Assistant is a web-based chatbot designed to help users understand **legal concepts, statutes, and legal documents**.
The chatbot can explain legal sections, provide references to laws, and help interpret uploaded legal documents.

This assistant is designed **only for legal information purposes** and does not provide legal advice.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🤖 AI-powered legal assistant
* 📄 Upload and analyze **PDF and DOCX legal documents**
* 🖼️ Extract text from **images (JPG, PNG) using OCR**
* 🔍 Ask questions related to uploaded documents
* ⚖️ Restricted to **legal-domain questions only**
* 🔒 Masks sensitive data such as:

  * Aadhaar numbers
  * PAN numbers
  * Phone numbers
  * Email addresses
* 💬 Interactive chatbot interface
* 🆕 Start a **new chat session anytime**

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* **HTML**
* **CSS**
* **JavaScript**
* **Voiceflow Chatbot**
* **PDF.js** (PDF text extraction)
* **Mammoth.js** (DOCX text extraction)
* **Tesseract.js** (OCR for images)

---

## 💡 How It Works

1. Upload a legal document (PDF, DOCX, JPG, PNG)
2. The system extracts text from the document
3. Sensitive information is automatically masked
4. Users ask legal questions through the chatbot
5. The chatbot responds using legal knowledge and document context

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

* This chatbot **does not provide legal advice**
* It is designed for **educational and informational purposes only**
